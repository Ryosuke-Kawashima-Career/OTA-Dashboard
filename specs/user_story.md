# User Story and Requirements

## User Story

**As a** president of an Online Travel Agency (OTA), supported by employees across strategy, finance, marketing, and product teams
**I want to** have a world-map based dashboard that benchmarks our company against rivals and against each regional market's growth rate, with historical market-share trajectories
**So that** the entire organization can move beyond the "revenue went up / revenue went down" debate and instead make decisions grounded in our true competitive position, ultimately lifting business performance and stock price for our institutional investors.

This tool must embody the principle of *know the enemy and know yourself, and you will not be imperiled in a hundred battles*. It must therefore answer two questions simultaneously, for every region and every time period:

1. **Know yourself:** Did our growth beat the regional market growth rate? Is our market share rising or falling?
2. **Know the enemy:** Which rivals are winning or losing share? What strategies (especially AI-driven) are they executing?

The dashboard must be usable not only by the president but by many employees company-wide as a shared reference for KPIs and strategy formulation. The system must explain the *meaning* of the data — financial statements of our company and rivals, and market growth per region — so that non-analyst employees can interpret it correctly.
